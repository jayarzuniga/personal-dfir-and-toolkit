## Automated Analysis

Automated Analysis is the process of using specialized tools and platforms to rapidly parse, decode, and investigate email messages and their components with minimal manual effort. These tools consolidate multiple analysis tasks — such as header parsing, attachment extraction, URL identification, and IOC enrichment — into a streamlined workflow, enabling security analysts to triage and respond to threats more efficiently.

Automated analysis tools are particularly valuable in high-volume environments such as security operations centers (SOCs), where analysts must process large numbers of user-reported phishing emails or alerts in a timely manner.

### Objectives of Automated Analysis

* Accelerate the triage and investigation of suspicious emails and files
* Automatically parse and decode email headers, bodies, and attachments
* Extract and enrich indicators of compromise with minimal manual effort
* Decode obfuscated or encoded content such as Base64 and hex strings
* Support repeatable and consistent analysis workflows across the SOC

### Common Information Analyzed

* Email header parsing and authentication results
* Embedded URLs, domains, and IP addresses
* Attachment metadata and extracted content
* Encoded and obfuscated data within email bodies and scripts
* Consolidated IOC extraction and enrichment results
