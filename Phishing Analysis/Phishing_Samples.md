## Phishing Samples

Phishing Samples repositories and platforms provide collections of real-world phishing emails, URLs, and associated artifacts that security researchers, detection engineers, and analysts can use for threat intelligence, rule development, and training purposes. Access to phishing samples enables teams to study attacker techniques, improve detection capabilities, and validate security controls against known malicious content.

These resources are commonly used in threat hunting, security awareness training development, detection engineering, and academic research into phishing trends and attacker infrastructure.

### Objectives of Phishing Samples Analysis

* Study real-world phishing techniques, lures, and attacker infrastructure
* Develop and validate detection rules and email security controls
* Enrich threat intelligence with known phishing URLs and indicators
* Support security awareness training with authentic phishing examples
* Enable research into evolving phishing trends and campaign patterns

### Common Information Analyzed

* Phishing URLs and associated domains and IP addresses
* Email lure content, themes, and social engineering techniques
* Attachment types and embedded malicious content
* Indicators of compromise extracted from phishing samples
* Campaign patterns and threat actor attribution
